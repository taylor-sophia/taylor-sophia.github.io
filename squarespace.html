I like the freedom that Squarespace gives you when designing your website. With many templates to choose from, easy positioning, and an abundance of free media, Squarespace made it really easy for me to design a creative yet professional website.


Tips and Tricks

  • Have a vision in mind before starting. Squarespace offers a lot of templates, but the choice can be overwhelming if you're not sure how you want your project to turn out.
  • Navigate the website beforehand.
  • Take advantage of everything Squarespace offers. They have some great tools that allow you to make a professional looking website.
