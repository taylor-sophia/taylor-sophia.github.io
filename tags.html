A Traveler's Guide to Boise Idaho

By Sophia Taylor

Boise Idaho a beautiful place with lots of things to do!

Boise is the capital of Idaho and the largest city in the state. Click here for more information about the history of Boise.

Fun facts about Boise

  • Boise is home to Boise State University
  • A movie called The To Do List takes place in Boise
  • On New Year's Eve Boise does a potato drop, similar to NYC's ball drop
  • Aaron Paul who plays Jesse Pinkman on Breaking Bad is from Boise. He has also lived in another Idaho city, Twin Falls. I moved back to Idaho when I was 6 or 7 and afterward lived in a little town called Twin Falls and afterward moved to Boise.

Top 10 things to do in Boise

  1. Skiing, snowboarding and sledding at Bogus Basin Mountain
  2. World Center for Birds of Prey
  3. Boise Botanical Garden
  4. Hiking at Table Rock
  5. Walking the Boise Greenbelt
  6. Old Idaho Penitentiary
  7. Zoo Boise
  8. Boise State Football Game
  9. Lucky Peak State Park
    Lake for boating and camping
  10. MK Nature Center
